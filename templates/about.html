Welcome To School Of Chess Academy

Welcome to the School of Chess Academy, established in 2010 in the beautiful city of Srikakulam. Our academy is renowned for providing professional chess coaching and is led by the esteemed coach, Bheri Chinnarao. As an international FIDE-rated chess player, Coach Chinnarao brings a wealth of knowledge and experience to our students.

Since our inception, we have trained more than 3000 students, nurturing their chess skills and helping them achieve their full potential. We take great pride in maintaining high coaching standards and ensuring that our students receive the best possible training.

We take immense pride in our track record of producing accomplished chess players who have excelled at various levels, from local tournaments to national and international championships. Our students have gone on to represent their schools, districts, and even countries in prestigious chess events.

About Us
